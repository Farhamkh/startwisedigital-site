How We Work

1

You choose a package & complete payment

Select Starter, Standard, or Premium and check out securely through Stripe. I’m notified right away.

2

Personalized welcome email

You’ll get a tier-specific email with your upload link (images, text, logos), what’s included for your tier (iterations/consultation/redesign for Premium), available add-ons, and simple instructions to request extras.

3

Domain decision

Want a new domain? I’ll check the live price and send a quick payment link; I’ll purchase it under an account in your name. Already own one? I’ll connect it at launch.

4

I begin your Initial Build

Your static site prototype is created from your tier and assets. Typical delivery: 3 business days to 1 week.

5

Review & refine

Preview your prototype via email. Approve as-is, or use your included iteration(s). Premium can also use the free redesign credit (applies to the three pages in the tier).

6

Add-ons anytime

After you buy a tier, you can request extras—New Pages, New Sections, additional iterations, consultation time, or a redesign. I’ll send a payment link and complete the add-on after confirmation.

7

Launch & handoff

I deploy the site, connect your domain, and email a final handoff. Want a downloadable backup? I’ll provide one on request.
