How we work

  1. 1

    Tell me what you need

    Email me or use the request form to share your goal — usually a one-page static website, a simple landing page, or help connecting your domain. No technical knowledge needed.

  2. 2

    We plan together

    We’ll do a quick 15-minute call over WhatsApp or Google Meet to plan your Initial Build, sections, and content. We’ll also outline what fits the $100 package and what counts as paid add-ons like extra sections, iterations, or redesigns.

  3. 3

    Delivery with full control

    I build your static site, connect your domain if needed, and launch it using accounts in your name. After you approve the Initial Build, any further changes follow the iteration and redesign rules in the Terms of Service.
